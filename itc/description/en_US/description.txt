Want to keep track of all the TV shows and movies you watch ?
iCineast will help you with both.

Powerful search
This applications offers you the possibility to easily search for information on TV shows, movies and even on actors.

Track your progress
Did it ever happen to you that you forgot which episode of a show you seen last ? With iCineast you can track every single episode.

Sync with all iOS devices
Via iCloud you can sync all of your data in order to keep them up-to-date on all of your devices.

Notifications
Get notified when a new episode of your favorite show is airing.

Statistics
Ever wondered how many time of your life you spent watching movies or tv shows ? iCineast will give you a nice little statistic about that.

Trailer, Multiple Themes, Ratings, ...
You can also watch trailers, teasers, ratings of movies and TV shows, change the appearance of the application and much more. 

We really hope you like this application.
Enjoy!
